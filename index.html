Our Story

Mami Hachi was made in 2026 from the increased love and appreciation of Japanese food and culture, along with the want to incorporate my own culture as well.

The name is a play on words, "mami", meaning mom, and "hachi" is the number 8. So mom ate, like she made really good food or actually just ate.

Each dish has a little mix of the tradtional Japanese foods and Latin flavors.
